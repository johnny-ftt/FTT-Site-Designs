Principal (AI-Augmented Strategist)

Remote Full-Time Strategic Leadership

About This Role

You'll own complete client outcomes and strategic direction—not just delivery, but the why behind what we build. This is a founder-level role where you combine deep strategic thinking with AI-augmented analysis to understand client businesses, identify opportunities, and guide delivery teams toward the right outcomes. You're the bridge between client vision and technical execution, ensuring we build the right things, not just things right.

What You'll Do

  • Own complete client relationships from initial engagement through delivery and ongoing optimization—you're accountable for outcomes, not tasks
  • Use AI tools to rapidly analyze client businesses, competitive landscapes, market positioning, and strategic opportunities—turning weeks of research into days
  • Define what success looks like for each client engagement and create strategic roadmaps that align technical delivery with business outcomes
  • Guide Pre-Sales AI Specialists on what to build for prospects—translating business requirements into technical direction
  • Lead AI-Augmented Developers and ensure delivery stays aligned with strategic intent as requirements evolve
  • Identify opportunities where AI can transform client workflows, not just automate existing processes
  • Make high-stakes decisions about scope, priorities, and trade-offs—balancing speed with strategic value
  • Build long-term client partnerships based on trust, results, and continuous strategic guidance
  • Contribute to our methodology and playbooks—capture what works and strengthen our strategic foundation

What You Bring

  • 7+ years in strategic consulting, product leadership, or senior client-facing roles where you owned business outcomes, not just deliverables
  • Deep understanding of digital business strategy, e-commerce, content strategy, and how technology creates competitive advantage
  • Strong technical literacy—you don't need to code, but you understand modern web architecture, CMS platforms, and what's technically feasible
  • Proven ability to use AI for strategic analysis—using LLMs for research, competitive analysis, content strategy, and rapid synthesis of complex information
  • Track record of identifying what really matters vs. what's just noise—you cut through complexity and focus teams on high-impact work
  • Exceptional client communication skills—you can explain complex technical and strategic concepts clearly and build trust quickly
  • Experience leading cross-functional teams autonomously—you know how to guide delivery without micromanaging
  • Strategic judgment about when to push clients, when to listen, and how to navigate ambiguity productively
  • Entrepreneurial mindset—you think like an owner, not a consultant, and you're comfortable with high autonomy and accountability
  • Curiosity about how AI changes strategic work—not just tactical automation, but fundamentally different approaches to analysis and decision-making

Ideal Background

  • Experience at top-tier consulting firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) or strategic digital agencies
  • Product leadership roles where you owned outcomes and P&L, not just roadmaps
  • Client-facing roles at Optimizely, Contentful, or similar enterprise platforms
  • Startup experience where you wore multiple hats and owned end-to-end outcomes
  • Track record of winning and growing strategic client relationships

Why This Role Matters

  • Principals are the foundation of our model—without strategic ownership, speed becomes chaos and delivery becomes commodity work
  • You're proving that AI enables one person to do strategic work that traditionally required teams of consultants
  • Every client success under your leadership strengthens our reputation and creates referenceable outcomes
  • You're defining what AI-augmented strategic consulting looks like—this is frontier work with massive leverage
  • This role has direct equity upside and path to partnership as the company grows
