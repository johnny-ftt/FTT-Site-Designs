AI-Augmented Developer

Remote Full-Time

About This Role

You'll take working prototypes from our pre-sales team and evolve them into production-grade systems. This isn't about maintaining code—it's about taking momentum from the sales process and carrying it through to launch and beyond. You'll work directly with clients, own technical outcomes, and use AI to move at speeds traditional developers can't match. Strong judgment, technical depth, and the ability to work autonomously are essential.

What You'll Do

  • Take rapid prototypes and transform them into production systems—adding robustness, performance, and polish without losing momentum
  • Build complex integrations with third-party APIs, payment systems, analytics platforms, and enterprise infrastructure
  • Implement advanced Optimizely features like personalization, A/B testing, content workflows, and custom functionality that pushes the platform's capabilities
  • Optimize for performance, accessibility, and SEO—not as afterthoughts, but as core requirements from day one
  • Work directly with clients to understand requirements, propose solutions, and deliver quickly without sacrificing quality
  • Use AI tools to accelerate development—but know when to override them, when to trust them, and how to structure work for maximum AI leverage
  • Build reusable components and patterns that strengthen our foundation—every project should make the next one easier
  • Own client relationships post-launch—you're not just building, you're ensuring success and continuous improvement

What You Bring

  • 5+ years of professional web development experience, with a track record of shipping production systems that actually work at scale
  • Deep expertise with React, Next.js, TypeScript, and modern frontend tooling—you know the ecosystem well enough to make good architectural decisions quickly
  • Experience with headless CMS platforms, particularly Optimizely, including content modeling, GraphQL APIs, and Visual Builder customization
  • Strong understanding of web performance optimization, accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1+), and modern SEO practices
  • Proven ability to use AI coding assistants effectively—you're productive with Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, or similar tools
  • Excellent judgment about when to build custom solutions vs. use existing packages, when to optimize vs. ship, and when to refactor vs. iterate
  • Ability to work autonomously, own client relationships, and manage your own time across multiple projects
  • Experience with modern deployment pipelines, monitoring, and DevOps practices
  • Strong communication skills—you can explain technical decisions to non-technical stakeholders clearly

Why This Role Matters

  • You're proving that AI-augmented development can deliver better results faster—not just prototypes, but production systems clients bet their business on
  • Every project strengthens our reusable foundations, making you and the entire team more effective over time
  • You own the full cycle from prototype to production to ongoing optimization—complete ownership, complete accountability
  • Direct client relationships mean you see the impact of your work immediately and build long-term partnerships
  • You're defining what AI-augmented development looks like in practice, not just theory
