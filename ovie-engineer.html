Ovie Platform Engineer

Remote Full-Time

About This Role

Build and evolve Ovie, the AI assistant that makes our entire approach sustainable. Ovie isn't a side product—it's the system that enables us to deliver fast and support clients continuously without burning out our team. You'll design AI-powered monitoring, optimization, and support systems that work 24/7, learn from every interaction, and make both clients and our team more effective. This is applied AI work with immediate, measurable impact.

What You'll Do

  • Design and build AI-powered monitoring systems that watch site performance, security, content quality, and user experience continuously
  • Develop intelligent agents that analyze patterns, identify issues, and generate actionable recommendations—not just alerts
  • Create automated workflows for content optimization, SEO improvements, performance tuning, and security monitoring that actually work in production
  • Build deep integrations with analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel), CMS systems (Optimizely), deployment pipelines, and monitoring tools
  • Design conversational interfaces that let clients interact with Ovie naturally—asking questions, getting explanations, approving changes
  • Implement learning systems that capture delivery knowledge, improve recommendations over time, and make Ovie smarter with each client interaction
  • Work directly with clients to understand what support they actually need vs. what they think they need
  • Balance automation with human judgment—Ovie should be helpful, not presumptuous

What You Bring

  • Strong full-stack development skills with Node.js, Python, or similar—you can build backend systems, APIs, and integrations quickly
  • Hands-on experience working with LLM APIs (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, etc.) in production environments, not just experiments
  • Deep understanding of web performance monitoring, optimization techniques, and how modern sites actually work under the hood
  • Solid grasp of AI/ML concepts, prompt engineering, and how to build reliable systems on top of probabilistic models
  • Experience building automation and monitoring systems that run reliably without constant babysitting
  • Ability to design systems that balance automation with human oversight—knowing when to act automatically and when to ask first
  • Understanding of SEO, analytics, content strategy, and digital marketing—enough to build tools that actually help
  • Product thinking—you don't just build what's requested, you understand the underlying problem and solve it well
  • Comfort with ambiguity—Ovie is still evolving, and you'll help define what it becomes

Why This Role Matters

  • Ovie is what makes our fast-delivery model sustainable—without it, we'd need to scale headcount linearly, which kills the economics
  • You're building systems that make both clients and our team more effective—true leverage, not just automation for automation's sake
  • Every improvement to Ovie benefits all clients simultaneously—your work compounds across the entire client base
  • You're defining what AI-powered support looks like in practice, creating a category that doesn't really exist yet
  • This role becomes more important and more interesting as AI capabilities improve—you're positioned at the frontier
